Guava

Guava leaves and fruit are rich in fiber, which aids digestion and relieves constipation.Guava leaf extract may help lower blood sugar levels, making it beneficial for diabetics.The fruit is rich in potassium and antioxidants, which help lower blood pressure and cholesterol levels

Mango

Mango contains enzymes like amylases, which help break down carbohydrates and improve digestion.Rich in vitamin C, A, and E, mango strengthens the immune system.Mango contains potassium and magnesium, which help regulate blood pressure.

Gulmohar

The bark and leaves are used to reduce inflammation and swelling.Traditional remedies apply Gulmohar leaf paste externally on wounds and swelling.Used to treat skin infections and prevent bacterial growth.

Jujuba

Jujuba seeds contain saponins and flavonoids, which have sedative properties.Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, which help strengthen the immune system.The fruit is used to treat constipation due to its high fiber content.

Mesua ferrea

Extracts from the flowers and leaves have antimicrobial effects, effective against bacterial and fungal infections.The bark decoction is used to treat diarrhea, dysentery, and indigestion.Mesua ferrea contains flavonoids and other bioactive compounds that promote heart health.

Indian goosberry

Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, Amla strengthens the immune system.Amla promotes gut health by enhancing digestion and reducing acidity.Contains polyphenols that help lower blood sugar levels, making it beneficial for diabetes management.Amla oil is widely used to strengthen hair, prevent hair fall, and reduce dandruff.

Golden cane palm

While not directly medicinal, Golden Cane Palm is known for its air-purifying properties.It helps remove toxins like formaldehyde and benzene from the air, promoting better respiratory health.The presence of indoor Golden Cane Palm plants is believed to have a calming effect, reducing stress and promoting mental well-being.

Indian coral tree

The bark and leaves are used to treat inflammation and pain.Traditionally applied as a poultice for swelling, arthritis, and joint pain.The leaf paste is applied to wounds, cuts, and boils to promote healing.Has antimicrobial properties that prevent infections.

Spanish elm

The leaves and bark are used to treat wounds, cuts, and skin infections.Contains antimicrobial properties that help prevent infections.

Jackfruit

Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants, jackfruit strengthens the immune system.Helps the body fight against infections and free radical damage.The fruit is high in dietary fiber, promoting healthy digestion and preventing constipation.Jackfruit seeds are used in traditional medicine to treat diarrhea and indigestion.
