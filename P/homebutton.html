Side bar about us

Dibrugarh University, located in the lush region of Assam, is home to a rich diversity of plant life that reflects the unique biodiversity of Northeast India. The university's Botany department emphasizes the study and conservation of this natural wealth, offering programs that delve into plant diversity, physiology, taxonomy, and ecology. With a focus on the region's endemic flora, students and researchers explore the potential of plants for sustainable development and human welfare. The campus itself is a green haven, fostering an environment conducive to both academic pursuits and ecological awareness. Through its curriculum and research initiatives, Dibrugarh University plays a vital role in understanding and preserving the botanical treasures of the region.
